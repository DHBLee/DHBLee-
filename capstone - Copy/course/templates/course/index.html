Welcome to my Course!

In this course, you'll explore three main sections: Strumming, Plucking, and Fingerstyle. We will begin with strumming, which serves as the foundation and is the easiest technique, then progress to plucking, and finally to the more advanced fingerstyle.

But of course, you have the flexibility to choose your starting point based on your comfort level.

Your progress will be tracked across these three sections, ensuring you stay motivated and informed on your learning journey.
